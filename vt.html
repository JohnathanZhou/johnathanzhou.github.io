Founder

For 2 years years in high school, I founded and worked on a nonprofit education platform for connecting students to free tutoring. I managed recruiting, marketing, & website development.

I started the organization out of a need for accessible tutoring that I saw in high school. I had numerous friends who were struggling in school but could not afford a tutor. I decided to address this need by creating a platform to connect capable volunteers to struggling students. I also recruited/led a team of 20 tutors to manage this.

Overall, I felt that this work in high school was some of my most meaningful. I managed to receive ~10,000 views on my site and help over 50 clients.
