Investment Analyst Intern, Summer 2018

Slice Capital launched their platform in May 2018, and I knew I wanted to be a part of it. I messaged Rohan Shah, the founder, and asked how I could get involved. He put me in touch with the head of growth, and I consequently spent part of my summer scouting startups in the Bay.

Over the summer, I maintained contact with the head of business development, Dylan Yuska, & sent him weekly reports on different startups that I had scouted and identified as good fits. I created my own valuation models based on previous funding, product type, & market fit.

Overall, I analyzed 60+ different seed-stage companies and built up my understanding of venture capital and crowdfunding.
