Software Engineering Intern, Summer 2017

Two summers ago, I worked at Codelancer, a startup that works on freelance software projects. While there, I worked in a team setting to build an “Uber for phone repairs” for a remote client.

We had a remote client who owned numerous phone repair stores and wanted to automate the process. We built an application that utilized UberRush API, Postmates API, and more to allow his customers to easily place an order and have it be delivered to the closest store. I worked on a lot of backend development, ranging from hashing user passwords with Bcrypt to error handling with Uber/Postmates API’s.

Overall, my team finished the application in half the expected time and led the client to receive $1000 in revenue upon launch. Additionally, I spearheaded the launch of the company website, which resulted in 150% more clientele.
