About

Hi! I'm Johnathan, a junior at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and minoring in Education.

Currently, I analyze seed-stage startups for Slice Capital, a crowfunding equity platform, and lead technology initiatives for ANova, UC Berkeley's leading computer science service club. I also help teach a digital marketing class at the Haas School of Business. I have a deep interest in the intersection of tech and social good and hope to work as a product manager in the future!
